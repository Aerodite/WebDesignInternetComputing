Blueberry Yogurt

How to make a decadent blueberry yogurt

Ingredients

  • 225g blueberries
  • 25g caster sugar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 500g Greek yogurt

Directions

  • Add the blueberries, sugar, and lemon juice to a pan
  • Let the mixture simmer for 10 minutes
  • Let the mixture cool until 25C
  • Add the yogurt to a bowl
  • Add the blueberry mixture to the yogurt
  • Mix the yogurt and blueberry mixture

**Please make sure your measuring tools use metric! Thank you.
Typical electric stove ranges would be Medium for this recipe
