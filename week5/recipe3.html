Beef Wellington

How to make the best Beef Wellington

Ingredients

  • 700g beef fillet
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp English mustard
  • 500g pack puff pastry

Directions

  • Heat oven to 200C
  • Rub the beef with oil and season with salt and pepper
  • Sear the beef in a hot pan for 30 seconds on each side
  • Brush the beef with mustard
  • Roll out the pastry and wrap the beef in it
  • Brush the pastry with egg yolk
  • Bake for 20 minutes
  • Let the beef rest for 10 minutes

**Please make sure your measuring tools use metric! Thank you.
Oven temperatures can safely range from 180-250, cook times will vary however.
