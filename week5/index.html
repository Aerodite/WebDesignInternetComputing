Croissant

How to make a croissant (with butter)

Ingredients

  • 500g flour mixture, plus extra for dusting (5% of flour mixture should contain yeast)
  • 10g salt
  • 80g caster sugar
  • 2oz butter, melted

Directions

  • Add the flour, salt, sugar and butter to a bowl. (with 200ml water)
  • Let the yeast rise for 4 minutes
  • Knead the dough for 10 minutes
  • Let the dough rise for 1 hour
  • Roll the dough into a rectangle, and subsequently fold it into thirds
  • Put in the oven

**Please make sure your measuring tools use metric! Thank you.
Also oven should be 150C
